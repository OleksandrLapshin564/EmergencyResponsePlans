Flood and Earthquake Safety

Floods and earthquakes are natural disasters that can cause widespread destruction. Awareness and preparedness significantly improve survival chances.

Flood Safety:

  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Disconnect electrical appliances to reduce shock risk.
  • Prepare a waterproof emergency kit with essentials.

Earthquake Safety:

  • During Shaking: Drop, Cover, and Hold On under a sturdy table.
  • Stay indoors until the shaking stops.
  • Avoid standing near windows, shelves, or heavy objects.
  • After the Quake: Check for injuries, fires, and structural damage.
  • Be prepared for aftershocks.

Knowledge and quick action can reduce harm and help communities recover faster.
