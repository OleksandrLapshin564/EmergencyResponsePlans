Emergency Contacts

In any emergency, quick access to the right contacts is critical. Keeping a list of emergency numbers ensures timely help and coordinated response.

Essential Emergency Contacts:

  • Police: 101
  • Fire Department: 101
  • Ambulance: 103
  • Local Emergency Management Office: Add local number
  • Utility Companies: Gas, electricity, and water provider hotlines
  • Family / Workplace Contacts: Identify key people to notify during emergencies

Keeping this information visible and easily accessible at home, work, or on a mobile device can save valuable time and lives in critical situations.
