Fire Safety Measures

Fires can spread quickly and become life-threatening within minutes. Fire safety measures are essential in homes, workplaces, and public buildings.

Fire Prevention Tips:

  • Install Smoke Alarms: Test them regularly and replace batteries as needed.
  • Avoid Overloading Circuits: Prevent electrical fires by not overusing outlets.
  • Safe Cooking: Never leave stoves unattended while in use.
  • Store Flammables Safely: Keep fuels, chemicals, and matches out of reach.

In Case of Fire:

  • Stay calm and alert others.
  • Use fire extinguishers if the fire is small and manageable.
  • Crawl low under smoke to avoid inhalation.
  • Never use elevators during evacuation.
  • Call emergency services immediately.
