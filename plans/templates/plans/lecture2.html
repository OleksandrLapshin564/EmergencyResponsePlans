Disaster Preparedness

Disaster preparedness involves planning and taking proactive measures to protect lives and property before an emergency occurs.

Key Steps in Preparedness:

  1. Risk Assessment: Identify hazards in your area.
  2. Emergency Plan: Include evacuation routes and meeting points.
  3. Emergency Kit: Stock essential items like water, food, first aid kit, flashlight, batteries.
  4. Training & Drills: Practice safety drills with family or team members.
  5. Stay Informed: Monitor news, weather alerts, and official notifications.
